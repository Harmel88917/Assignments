James Gosling

Curriculum vitae



PERSONNAL DETAILS

Names: James Gosling

Date of Birth: May 19, 1955

Nationality: Canadian

Gender: Male

Marital Status: Married

Address: San Francisco Bay Area, California, U.S.

Education and career

* Bachelor of Science from the University of Calgary.

* Ph.D. from Carnegie Mellon University.

* Between 1984 and 2010, Gosling was with Sun Microsystems.
He is known as the father of the Java programming language.
He got the idea for the Java VM while writing a program to port software from a
PERQ by translating Perq Q-Code to VAX assembler and emulating the hardware.

Honors

* 2002: he was awarded The Economist Innovation Award.

* 2002: he was awarded The Flame Award USENIX Lifetime Achievement Award.

* 2007: he was made an Officer of the Order of Canada.

* 2013: he became a fellow of the Association for Computing Machinery.

* 2015: awarded IEEE John von Neumann Medal.
